# Student Role

Students are the primary users of the Mentor LMS platform, accessing and engaging with educational content created by instructors. The student interface is designed to provide an intuitive and effective learning experience.

## Student Dashboard

After logging in, students are greeted with a personalized dashboard that displays their enrolled courses and account information.

<!-- ![Student Dashboard](/configuration/student-dashboard.png) -->

The student interface includes:

- **Profile Section**: Displays student name and email
- **My Courses**: List of enrolled courses with status indicators
- **My Exams**: Access to enrolled exams and exam results
- **Navigation Menu**: Easy access to all student features
- **Language Selector**: Choose your preferred language for the interface

## Student Navigation

The sidebar menu provides easy access to all student features:

- **My Courses**: View and access your enrolled courses
- **My Exams**: View and take enrolled exams
- **Wishlist**: Saved courses and exams for future enrollment
- **My Profile**: Manage your personal information
- **Settings**: Configure account preferences
- **Appearance**: Customize interface display options
- **Language**: Select your preferred platform language
- **Logout**: Securely exit your account

## Course Enrollment

### Enrolling in a Course

1. Select a course of interest
2. Review the course details:

   - Course description and objectives
   - Curriculum overview
   - Instructor background
   - Student reviews and ratings
   - Price and enrollment options

3. Add to cart or enroll directly
4. Complete the payment process (for paid courses)
5. Receive confirmation and immediate access to course content

### Managing Enrollments

- **Course Access**: Enter enrolled courses from the dashboard
- **Learning Progress**: Track completion status across all courses
- **Certificates & Marksheets**: Download earned certificates and academic records upon course completion
- **Enrollment History**: View record of all course registrations
- **Exam Enrollments**: Manage standalone exam registrations separately from courses

## Learning Experience

### Accessing Course Content

The enhanced student course management system provides organized access to all learning materials:

- **Module Viewer**: Navigate through course modules with lesson completion tracking
- **Video Lessons**: Watch instructor-created video content
- **Reading Materials**: Access text lessons and supplementary resources
- **Lesson Resources**: Download PDFs, worksheets, and other materials uploaded by instructors
- **Resources Hub**: Centralized access to all course materials with easy download options
- **Live Classes**: Join scheduled live sessions directly from your dashboard
- **Real-time Progress**: Visual indicators showing your course completion percentage

### Assessments and Progress

- **Quizzes**: Complete knowledge checks throughout the course
- **Quiz Management**: View quiz results and submission status in organized interface
- **Assignments**: Submit required coursework for instructor review
- **Assignment Hub**: Track all submissions and view results in one place
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual indicators of course completion with real-time updates
- **Achievement Badges**: Earn recognition for course milestones (if enabled)

### Interaction and Community

- **Discussion Forums**: Participate in course-specific conversations
- **Q&A**: Ask questions related to course content
- **Peer Interaction**: Connect with fellow students
- **Instructor Communication**: Direct messaging with course instructors
- **Reviews & Feedback**: Share course experiences and suggestions

## Exam System

### Taking Exams

Mentor LMS features a comprehensive standalone exam system:

- **Exam Catalog**: Browse and enroll in exams by category
- **Exam Wishlist**: Save exams for future enrollment
- **Exam Purchase**: Buy exam access through integrated payment gateways
- **Coupon Codes**: Apply discount codes during exam checkout

### Exam Taking Experience

Professional examination interface with advanced features:

- **7 Question Types**: Answer MCQ, Multiple Select, Matching, Fill in the Blanks, Ordering, Short Answer, and Listening questions
- **Countdown Timer**: Track remaining time with auto-submit when time expires
- **Question Navigator**: View all questions with status indicators (answered, unanswered, marked for review)
- **Mark for Review**: Flag questions to revisit before submission
- **Fullscreen Mode**: Take exams in distraction-free environment
- **Navigation Protection**: Receive warnings before accidentally leaving the exam
- **Auto-Save**: Your answers are automatically saved as you progress

### Exam Results & History

- **Instant Results**: View detailed score breakdown immediately after submission (for auto-graded questions)
- **Performance Analytics**: Analyze your performance by question type
- **Attempt History**: Track all exam attempts with best score highlighting
- **Pass/Fail Status**: Clear visual indicators of exam results
- **Review & Rating**: Rate and review exams after completion
- **Certificates**: Download exam completion certificates when applicable

## Becoming an Instructor

Students can apply to become instructors directly from their dashboard:

1. Click on **Become an Instructor** in the sidebar
2. Complete the application form with required information:
   - **Resume**: Upload your resume/CV file
   - **Skills**: Enter relevant teaching skills as tags
   - **Biography**: Write about yourself and your expertise
3. Submit your application for review

Once approved, you'll gain access to instructor features while maintaining your student account.

As a student on Mentor LMS, you have access to a wealth of knowledge from expert instructors around the world. The platform features a clean, intuitive interface designed to support your learning journey from enrollment to course or exam completion.
